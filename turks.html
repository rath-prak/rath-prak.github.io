Turk’s Chicken

One of New Zealand’s premium chicken brands.

Turk’s had recently made a shift to certified free-range farming, the task was to update their online presence and create a site more in line with the premium free-range chicken industry, with an important shift from a wholesaler supply channel to a direct-to-consumer solution. As a core member of the design team, I was tasked with developing the UI/UX for the new site.
