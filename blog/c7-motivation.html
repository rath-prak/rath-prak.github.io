Wednesday 2nd March, 2016

How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

Most people seek happiness through pleasure which is not very sustainable because it can come and go and last for very short periods. We spend most of our lives chasing pleasure falsely believing that it's true happiness and spend less time trying to find a higher purpose which can lead to true and sustainable happiness. People should spend more time finding a higher purpose and less on seeking pleasure.
Most people don't enjoy going to work because the don't have a purpose. The key to enjoying work is to find your own higher purpose, if you know what you value most and what is most meaningful to you and then work towards it. This then becomes a source of sustainable happiness.

From the 'discovering values and higher purpose' exercise.
