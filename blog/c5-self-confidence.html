Friday 22nd January, 2016

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Daniel Goleman defines “Self-awareness as a neutral mode that maintains self-reflectiveness even in the midst of turbulent emotions.” Where as Jon Kabat-Zinn defines mindfulness as “Mindfulness means paying attention in a particular way: on purpose, in the present moment, and nonjudgmentally.” Both are aim to control emotion so they are essentially the same. Both are trainable.

Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling).

I tried Journaling, I wrote a journal for 3mins before I went to sleep using three prompts: What motivates me is, I am inspired by, Tomorrow, I aspire to.

What was your experience of it?

I actually quite enjoyed it, what I found was that I was about things that I had already thought about and things that I didn't think would inspire me. Also when I woke up in the mornings I still had the thoughts from the previous night still in my head.

How did you feel?

I felt a little more motivated.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not me" section?

Being self confident is not about have a big ego or overestimating your abilities, its about reaching inside yourself and being aware of ones strengths and weakness and being able to control them.
